Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This notice describes what information is collected when you visit sesinteractive.com or contact us, and how that information is used.

01 Who we are

Someone Else's Shoes Interactive LLC ("SES Interactive," "we," "us") is a Michigan-based independent game studio. We can be contacted at hello@sesinteractive.com.

02 What this site collects

The sesinteractive.com website is a static information site. It does not set cookies, run analytics, collect personal information through forms or logins, track your browsing activity, or display advertisements.

When you visit, your browser exchanges some technical information with the server hosting the site (GitHub Pages), including your IP address, the page you requested, your user agent string, and timing. This is standard for any web browsing and is logged by GitHub for operational and security purposes per their own privacy policy.

03 Third-party services

To display the site, your browser loads a few resources from third parties:

  • Google Fonts. Font files are loaded from fonts.googleapis.com when you open a page. This means Google's servers see your IP address and basic browser information. We don't have control over what Google logs from these requests. See policies.google.com/privacy for Google's privacy policy.

These are the only third-party resources currently loaded by the site.

04 If you email us

If you send a message to hello@sesinteractive.com or any other studio email address, that email is received and stored in Google Workspace, operated by Google LLC. We retain the content of correspondence as needed to respond, maintain records, and prevent abuse. We don't share your email content with anyone outside the studio without your permission.

05 External links

The site contains links to other websites, including our games' product sites, the LinkedIn profile of our founder, and potentially press coverage or partner sites in the future. Once you click a link to a third-party site, that site's own privacy policy applies. We don't control what those sites do.

06 Children

The site is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we've inadvertently received information from a child under 13, contact us and we'll delete it.

07 Your rights

If we have information about you, typically because you've emailed us or signed up for one of our game programs, you can:

  • Request a copy of what we have
  • Request that we correct or delete it
  • Request that we stop using it for any purpose

Email hello@sesinteractive.com with the request and we'll respond within a reasonable time. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under laws like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We honor these regardless of where you're located.

08 Game-specific programs

If you participate in a closed playtest, beta program, or other testing program for one of our games, additional terms specific to that program will apply. Those terms cover what information is collected as part of the program and how it's used.

09 Changes to this notice

We may update this notice as the studio's activities evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be noted at the top of the page or, where appropriate, communicated by email.

10 Contact

Questions about this notice or your information: hello@sesinteractive.com.
